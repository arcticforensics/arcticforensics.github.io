Create a writable, pseudo file copy of physical drive 2 on the Desktop
ro2w \\.\physicaldrive2

Create a writable virtual hard drive of write-protected physical drive 5 on the Desktop
raw2VHD \.\physicaldrive5 \Users\user\Desktop\MyVHD
ro2w \Users\user\Desktop\MyVHD\physicaldrive2.vhd

Create a writable virtual hard drive of the contents of a physical drive stored as an EWF (file.ewf) on the Desktop
ewf2raw file.ewf \Users\user\Desktop\MyEWF
raw2VHD \Users\user\Desktop\MyEWF\ewf1 \Users\user\Desktop\MyVHD
ro2w \Users\user\Desktop\MyVHD\ewf1.vhd
